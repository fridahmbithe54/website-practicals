About

History and Vision of the company

MHealth Kenya is the pioneer of mobile health technologies and initiatives bringing together a team of experts with a diversity of knowledge, experience, and a deep understanding of the Health sector.mHealth focuses on obtaining information immediately to diagnose illnesses, track diseases and provide timely information to the public in underserved countries. Mobile health (mHealth) is especially important in remote areas where doctors and nurses may not be present to provide treatment.
